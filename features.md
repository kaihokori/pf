# Features

* Base Body Stats: Height, Weight, Age, Gender
* Coaching Setup Information: Goal, Workout Frequency (or desired), Workout Experience, Equipment Access, Dietary Requirements or Injuries
* Weekly Progress Tracking: Check-In with Weight and Photo, Answer Questions (on a scale from 1 to 5) Regarding Energy, Hunger, and Sleep Quality, Show Muscle Highlights for that Week
* Macro Tracking: Calculate Daily Intake using ‘Mifflin St Jeor’ Formula (for calories and all major macros), Save Meals into Collections (including a “Favourites” collection), Day Separated Into Meal Times (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack/Other), Link to Macro Library API (combination of openfoodfacts.org and USDA FoodData Central), Water Tracking, Calorie Floor/Ceiling Warning, Easy Insert from Plan Function
* Workout Tracking: Rest Sets, Standard Sets, Failure Sets, Drop Sets, Reps, Rest Timer, Previous Stats on that Set From Previous Session, Show Volume Moved and Muscle Highlights for that Session
* Exercise Library: Library (filter by body part) Step-by-Step Video Exercises (with text and annotations), Progress Chart
* Recipe Library: Macros, Recipes (filter by meal type and allergies/dietary requirements), Add recipes
* Coaching Plans (Coach Side): Create, Duplicate, Edit, Assign to Users, and Delete Nutrition Plans (with optional high and low days) and Workout Plans, and Add, Edit and Remove Tips for individual clients. 
* Coaching Plan (Client Side): View Nutrition and Workout Plans, Create, Duplicate, Edit and Delete Own Nutrition and Workout Plans and View Given Tips
* Coaching Interaction (Coach Side): Messaging (with notifications), Schedule Meeting Session, Call Client Only (audio only or video depending on tier), Send and view Photos and Files
* Coaching Interaction (Client Side): View Client Profile (with all stats), Messaging (with notifications), View Scheduled Meeting, Accept Calls Only, Send and View Photos and Files
* Gamification: Leaderboard (determined by some calculated score), Streaks, Achievements (in form of badges)
* HealthKit Integration: Save Weight and Macros (and Water) Automatically, and Automatically Fetch and Save Step Count
* Statistics (Coach Side): When Viewing Each Client, See Status (Active, Ex-Client), Filter Client List (by goal, adherence, risk flags), View AI-Generated Insights and Suggested Talking Points (based on Weekly Progress and Other Tracking) with Button to Share to Client, View Graphical Stats for Workouts and Nutrition Tracking and View Animated Progress (collection of photos), Write Notes (not shared with client), View Red Flags (Injury History or Other Considerations)
* Statistics (Client Side): View Coach-Approved AI Insights, Graphical Stats for Workouts and Nutrition Tracking and View Animated Progress (collection of photos)
* Account Page (Coach Side): Change Name, Photo or Sign Out
* Account Page (Client Side): Change Name, Shuffle Profile Picture (pre-made avatars), Age, Gender, Height, Cancel Subscription (takes to page with instructions), Delete Account, Sign Out
* Notifications (Coach Side): Alert If Client Weekly Progress Missed, When Weekly Progress Submitted and When Client is Falling Behind
* Notifications (Client Side): Workout, Meal and Weekly Progress Check-In Reminders at Chosen Times of Day (determine during onboarding), If Not Logged a Workout/Meal in Days Send Nudge
* In-App Purchases: Subscription Plan with Three Tiers Taken Either Monthly, Every 6 Months or Every Year
* Other: Allow for Offline Nutrition and Workout Tracking with Later Syncing, Plan Templates, Support to Let the User Select One Coach out of Many on the App during Onboarding, Data Exporting, Onboarding (one question per screen, progress bar, smart defaults), Hide/Show tabs (like hiding workouts if you don’t workout), Themes, Disclaimers, Privacy Policy and T&Cs, Where Data is Sourced, App Support
